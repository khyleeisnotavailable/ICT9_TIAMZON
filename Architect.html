Architect | Designer I

  • An architectural designer is an entry-level professional who typically works within an architecture firm
    or business under the supervision of a fully certified architect.
  • Working with applications and computer-based programs to develop and initiate design plans. Designing
    various elements of buildings, infrastructures or other architectural elements.
  • Assisting team and client with initial design and planning. Using computer-aided draft (CAD) programs
    for developing designs of infrastructural blueprints.
