Nurse Anesthetist

  • Nurse anesthesiologists are advanced practice nurse who provides anesthesia and pain medication to patients before, during, and
    after surgery or other medical procedure.
  • We also monitor the patient's biological functions and coordinate their care with other medical professionals
  • We are not medical doctors! Nurse anesthetists should not be confused with anesthesiologists in that CRNAs are not medical doctors,
    but instead are advanced practice nurses with graduate degrees.
